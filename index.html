Gratitude 🙏

Write down three things you're grateful for. Here's the trick... the smaller, the dumber, the better.

1.
2.
3.

Examples: a zipper that works nice, the cool side of the pillow, a soft blanket, the sound that something makes, the way paper feels, how nice water is when you're thirsty, a satisfying button, a website you like, a comfy t-shirt, the way gravity keeps things from floating away, NHK, potential sharts that were only farts, a good sneeze, a good stretch, a good yawn, a good burp

Body Gratitude 🫁

Think of one body part that works, or even one that is struggling. Close your eyes and think about it for a minute, and all the cool times you've had. Thank the body part!

1.

Example: Think about your ears. How much cool music have they heard? Without ears, how would I even know how great Freddie's voice is or know the joy of David Bowie? Thanks, ears!

Bonus: Do that, but for a body part that's struggling. Thank it for trying its best.
